Revisiting the Arguments for Edge Computing Research

Abstract:

This article argues that low latency, high bandwidth, device proliferation, 
sustainable digital infrastructure, and data privacy and sovereignty 
continue to motivate the need for edge computing research even though its 
initial concepts were formulated more than a decade ago.
