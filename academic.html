Service Highlight

 "We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give." -- Winston Churchill

I have been contributing to various scientific communities in terms of editorship, reviewing, chairing, programme committee, and volunteering. As the co-founder of FCN series since 2014, I have been building and strengthening this international community to promote active collaborations between top European and Asian institutes, including Cambridge, Copenhagen, Delft, Helsinki, London, Munich, Oslo, Stockholm, Vienna, MPI, IMEC, EPFL, HKUST, Tsinghua, Peking, Fudan, and Chinese Academy of Science (CAS). I value the opportunities to work with international scholars of different backgrounds and cultures. Along the way, I visit, host and collaborate with many distinguished colleagues across Europe, Asia, and North America.

In computer science domain, I am an active member with chairing, executive and nomination duties for ACM, IEEE, and IETF.
